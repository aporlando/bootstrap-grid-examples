3.4. Nesting Grids

Nesting grids allows us to create more complex layouts.

Level 1: .col-sm-9

I may choose to add some content to this level.

Level 2: .col-xs-8 .col-sm-6

Then I can also organize content into columns within a row.

Level 2: .col-xs-4 .col-sm-6
Level1: .col-sm-3

I have a sidebar over here!

I could put all kinds of stuff in here - navigation, advertisements, click bait... The possibilities are endless!

The Code

<div class="row">
    <div class="col-sm-9">
        <div>
            Level 1: .col-sm-9
            <p> I may choose to add some content to this level.</p>
        </div>
        <div class="row">
            <div class="col-xs-8 col-sm-6">
                Level 2: .col-xs-8 .col-sm-6
                <p>Then I can also organize content into columns within a row.</p>
            </div>
            <div class="col-xs-4 col-sm-6">
                Level 2: .col-xs-4 .col-sm-6
                <img class="img-responsive" src="../images/yo-dawg.jpg" />
            </div>
        </div>
    </div>
    <div class="col-sm-3">
        <div>
            Level1: .col-sm-3
            <p>I have a sidebar over here!</p>
            <p>I could put all kinds of stuff in here - navigation, advertisements, click bait... The possibilities are endless!</p>
        </div>
    </div>
</div>
