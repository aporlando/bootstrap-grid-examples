3.1. Columns wrap within rows

If more than 12 columns are placed within a single row, each group of extra columns will, as one unit, wrap onto a new line.

col4
col4
col4
col8
col5

Since 9 + 5 = 13 > 12, this column gets wrapped onto a new line as one contiguous unit.
